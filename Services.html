Apple Services

AppleCare acts as an Apple-specific insurance program to protect against device damage and theft. While products come with a one-year limited warranty, users may want more coverage for a longer period.


App Store: The App Store is Apple's digital distribution platform for iOS, iPadOS, and macOS applications.
Users can download and install apps and games from a vast selection curated by Apple.

Apple Inc. is a renowned American multinational technology company with its headquarters located in Cupertino, California. Founded on April 1, 1976, by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, Apple has grown to become a global leader in the tech industry. The company is primarily known for its iconic line of consumer electronics, software, and services.

Apple's product lineup includes the immensely popular iPhone, which revolutionized the smartphone market with its sleek design and user-friendly interface. Alongside the iPhone, the iPad, a highly successful tablet device, has also garnered widespread acclaim for its versatility and performance.

Apple's commitment to innovation has been evident throughout its history. The company's Macintosh computers, with their cutting-edge features and design, have gained a dedicated user base. Additionally, the introduction of the iPod and iTunes revolutionized the way people listen to music, propelling Apple into the realm of digital media and entertainment.

Beyond hardware, Apple has developed and maintained various software ecosystems that have further solidified its position in the market. The iOS operating system powers iPhones and iPads, while macOS provides a seamless user experience for Mac computers.

Apple Music is a subscription-based music streaming service that offers a vast library of songs, albums, and playlists for users to enjoy on their iOS devices, Macs, and other platforms.

iMessage is Apple's proprietary messaging service that allows users to send text messages, photos, videos, and other media between Apple devices over Wi-Fi or cellular data.
